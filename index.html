Privacy Policy

Welcome to Lingulia! Your privacy is important to us. This page outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

    • Setting the correct time zone for delivering notifications.

How We Use Your Data

  • To send personalized notifications based on your preferences.

Data Protection

We prioritize the security of your data and implement measures to ensure it is protected from unauthorized access or misuse.
